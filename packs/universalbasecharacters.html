Universal Base Characters
August 2025
The Universal Base Characters Kit includes 6 game-ready character models in Superhero, Regular, and Teen proportions (male and female). Designed with optimized, animation-friendly topology and rigged with a Humanoid rig allowing easy retargeting in any engine. Mix and match 20 hairstyles, the Source version includes shaders to customize eye and skin colors allowing hundreds of unique combinations.
Features
6 Base models.
Game-ready topology optimized for animation.
20 Hairstyles to mix and match, along with customizable eye and skin color, allowing you to create a wide variety of unique characters.
Humanoid Rig compatible with retargeting in any engine, Source version includes the rigged .blend files.
Average 13k triangle count
Compatible with the Universal Animation Library.
Free to use in personal, educational and commercial projects. (CC0 License)
.FBX and .glTF formats, compatible with all engines.
